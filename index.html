SURVIVAL DRONE INVASION

A fast-paced, first-person experience set in a future taken over by deadly hi-tech drones. As humanity's only hope, it's your mission to identify this threat's origin and neutralize it. But are you ready to face an army whose single target is yourself?

Play on:

"Emitters is my 1st game project focused on a single player FPS survival drone invasion. The game is in continuous development since 2017 and released on STEAM. I hope you like it and see you on Emitters social networks."

Mickael Morgado (HYTEK94)

  • Emitters is an industry specialized in high-tech equipment for the army and for civilian protection
  • Since 2377, they've already deployed over 1 million robot units that prioritize human safety under any circunstances
  • But recently, many units have been hijacked and become extremely dangerous, even killing people inadvertently
  • "Emitters industry" is working hard to stop these occurrences, but even with additional tests and 24h surveillance, incidents are exponentially becoming more frequent and dangerous
  • These hijacks are the result of brute-force security breaches that somehow allow third-parties to take over targeted units, detaching them from emitter's control.
  • After ceasing shipping of their products on a global scale, emitters started creating the best military android to identify and neutralize this global threat - and that android is you!
"SAVE YOUR COMPANY. SAVE MANKIND"

  • EMP Blast

Collect all the Batteries that spawn in some waves of your training levels to activate all the Chargers on the map, recovering the arena electricity to escape the level.

Once recovered the EMP Tower will blast out all the surrounded enemies and stop the wave process of training.

Thoses training are created by Emitters Industry to test their Autonomous Anti-Invasion Proptotypes (AAIP) pushing you even more towards your own evolution.

Features

Batteries

The batteries are used to power up the chargers respectivly by their colors. They are going to spawn troughout enemy waves.

Chargers

By activating all chargers with batteries the emp tower will re-establish the main power of the level re-enabling the emergency door.

Emp Tower

EMPs are the ultimate emergency safety Emitters system that eliminate any defectuous high-tech droid/robot concentration in cities.
20171200 - "AAIP failed the tests but we are working hard to make the perfect one."

Turrets

Activate this sentry gun to automatically target and eliminate drones.

  • 30 seconds of activation
  • Accumulate EP on each Kill

Barrels

By shooting them, thoses gas barrels can save you in hard situations by exploding near enemies.

  • The closer enemies are, the more critical damage they take
  • Instantaneous explosion
  • Great explosion range

Airstrike

Maybe one of the most efficient Perk you can request from Emitters control center.

Call the airstrike support to frag out the entire map.

  • You can use it at the perfect timing to frag out all your following enemies.
  • By Modding it, the support will be stronger.

Drone

Emitters can send you a controllable drone fast and agile, allowing fast and large bursts of fire.

Efficient in close fights to protect your owner life as in far distance for strategically fast eliminations.

  • Rapid fire
  • Great handle and fast movements

Helicopter

Emitter's very own on-demand air strike unit.

  • Clear areas overcrowded with enemies
  • Strike enemies while you're busy somewhere else

COMD

Repurposed from deprecated AAIPs, COMDs can support you (and only you) in battle.

  • If you manage to call it multiple time, you can easily end up with a good team.
