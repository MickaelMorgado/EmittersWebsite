SURVIVAL DRONE INVASION

A fast-paced, first-person experience set in a future taken over by deadly hi-tech drones. As humanity's only hope, it's your mission to identify this threat's origin and neutralize it. But are you ready to face an army whose single target is yourself?

Watch Trailer Play on Steam

"Emitters is my 1st solo game dev project made on UE4 a single player FPS survival drone invasion game released on STEAM. The development started since 2017 learning from scratch, I hope you like it and see you on Emitters social networks."

Mickael Morgado (HYTEK94)

  • Emitters is an industry specialized in high-tech equipment for the army and for civilian protection
  • since 2377, they've already deployed over 1 million robot units that prioritize human safety under any circunstances
  • but recently, many units have been hijacked and become extremely dangerous, even killing people inadvertently
  • "emitters industry" is working hard to stop these occurrences, but even with additional tests and 24h surveillance, incidents are exponentially becoming more frequent and dangerous
  • these hijacks are the result of brute-force security breaches that somehow allow third-parties to take over targeted units, detaching them from emitter's control.
  • after ceasing shipping of their products on a global scale, emitters started creating the best military android to identify and neutralize this global threat - and that android is you!

"SAVE YOUR COMPANY. SAVE MANKIND"

  • Power up The Totem

Collect all the Batteries that spawn in some waves of your training levels to activat all the Chargers on the map, recovering the arena electricity to escape the level.

Once recovered the Totem will blast out all the surrounded enemies and stop the wave process of training.

Thoses training are created by Emitters Industry to test their Anti-Invasion Autonomous Proptotypes (AIAP) pushing you even more towards your own evolution.

[Features]

Batteries

The batteries are used to power up the chargers respectivly by their colors. They are going to spawn troughout your enemies level waves.

Chargers

By activating all the chargers with your batteries the totem will re-establish the main power of the level re-enabling the emergency door.

Totems

Totems are the ultimate emergency safety Emitters system that eliminate any defectuous high-tech droid/robot concentration in cities.
20171200 - Failed the tests but we are working hard to make the perfect AIAP

Drones

Emitters Industry builded some quadcopters for military security each one auto-controlled by AI but now they need to use them against you has your training protocol before possible World war.

Androids

Same as the Drones, Androids are created to secure multiple cities arround the world and even help people for daily routines.
