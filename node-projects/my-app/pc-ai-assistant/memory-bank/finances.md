# Financial Knowledge & Context

## Investment Principles
- Diversification reduces risk across different asset classes
- Long-term investing outperforms market timing strategies
- Compounding interest is the most powerful force in investing
- Risk and return are directly correlated in financial markets

## Market Understanding
- Stock markets reflect economic fundamentals over time
- Volatility is normal and should be embraced as an opportunity
- Market timing is extremely difficult, even for professionals
- Dollar-cost averaging reduces the impact of market timing

## Risk Management
- Never invest money you cannot afford to lose
- Emergency funds should cover 3-6 months of expenses
- Insurance protects against catastrophic financial losses
- Debt should be managed carefully, prioritizing high-interest obligations

## Wealth Building
- Consistent saving is more important than investment returns
- Multiple income streams provide financial stability
- Education and skills development increase earning potential
- Tax-advantaged accounts should be utilized when available

## Economic Concepts
- Inflation erodes purchasing power over time
- Interest rates influence borrowing costs and investment returns
- Supply and demand drive market prices
- Economic cycles affect different asset classes differently

## Financial Planning
- Retirement planning should start as early as possible
- Budgeting ensures income exceeds expenses
- Regular financial check-ups help maintain course
- Professional advice can be valuable for complex situations
